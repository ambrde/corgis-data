This website allows you to explore data on wind turbines in the US. You can do so in two ways:

Explore the Data by State Explore the Data by Year

Under "Data by State", you can see the total number of wind turbines and their capacity in your selected state, as well as additional resources on wind energy in that state. Under "Data by Year", you can see the total number of wind turbines that began operating in your selected year.

The data used on this website is from the CORGIS dataset, which can be found here. The data is collected from the United States Wind Turbine Database (USWTD). There is data on each individual wind turbine in the US--its location, height, electrical generation capacity, and more.

Check out this viewer from USWTD to explore some more!
